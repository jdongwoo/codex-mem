# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want `codex-mem` to be a respectful, inclusive, and constructive project for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional in discussions and reviews.
- Assume good intent and focus on technical substance.
- Give actionable feedback and avoid personal attacks.
- Welcome contributors at different experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
- Personal attacks or sustained hostile behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions

## Scope

This applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and reviews.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code.
If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, open a private discussion with maintainers via GitHub.

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participation for violations.
